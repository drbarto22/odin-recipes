Ingredents

  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Carrots
  • White Onion
  • Frozen Peas
  • Garlic
  • Salt adn Pepper
  • Cooked White Rice
  • Green Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Toasted Sesame Oil

    Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add egg, cook untill scrambled. Remove egg and set aside
  2. Add more butter ad carrot, onion, peas, and garlic. Season with salt and peper until tender. Add more butter and cook rice and green onions. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce. Continue to stir untill combined. saute for 3 mins then add the eggs back in.
