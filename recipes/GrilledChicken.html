Ingredents

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Cajuan Seasoning

    Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken long way also known as butterflying
  2. Add all the seasonings to the cuts of chicken making sure to coat them all
  3. Add butter to the pan and wait for it to melt
  4. Add the chicken to the pan cooking 5 mins each side
  5. Remove and let it rest for 5 mins before cutting
